Our Story

Why We Built This & Future Scopes

The Employable Humans was born from personal frustration and a powerful vision — that disability should never be a barrier to meaningful work.

21
2026 · The Beginning
Founded by team aplha in greater noida
After seeing such problem to the many peoples, Alpha — a NIET college Student — made an application organise interviews and help special abled peoples to find the job despite exceptional qualifications. we bulid it as a prototype only now.
22
2027 · First 100 Companies
Seed funding of ₹4 crore in future
We will onboarded our first 100 inclusive employer partners — from startups to Tata and Infosys. 600+ people will found jobs in our first year. We moved into a fully accessible office in Koramangala, Bengaluru.
23
2028· Lauching Phase
we will Launching in 12 differnt Indian cities
Expanded to Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, and 8 more cities. Introduced the Salary Negotiator tool — it will users negotiated ₹47 crore in collective salary increases. Won India's Startup of the Year for Social Impact.
24
2029 · AI & Scale
40,000+ seekers, 1,800+ companies
Launched our AI matching engine. Signed the Inclusive Employer Pledge with 200 Fortune 500 companies. Introduced sign-language video interviews and screen-reader-native job listings.
